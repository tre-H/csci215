BROOM: A Dirt-Lover's Paradise

    Instructions

  • Click the 'Start' button to begin the game
  • Bring your mouse cursor over the game to begin controlling it
  • There will be an increasing number of dirt piles to be swept after every level (can you make it to level 50?!)
  • You start with a total of 20 lives on the first level, to get used to the game
  • After Level 1, you will only be given 10 lives per level - THIS A CHALLENGE!
  • Once you inevitably fail, a 'Reset' button will appear
  • Take the janitorial challenge of a lifetime as many times as you please - Don't give up and have fun!
